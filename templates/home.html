Warehousing Apps

Applications

Slot profiles

Ask any warehouse manager and he will probably tell you that he is running out of space and is looking for more. However, by using optimal slot profiling, warehouse managers can maximize space utilization, making room for more product within the same available space.

Aisle designs

In a traditional warehouse, storage racks are arranged to create parallel picking aisles, perhaps with one or more cross aisles perpendicular to the picking aisles. Fishbone layouts challenge these two principles with the use of diagonal aisles and the orientation of picking aisles. As result, Fishbone layouts present a 20% less travel time.

Hotspot analysis

A heat map of pick-lines in the storage area helps you identify congested areas and if the picking and put-away operations are properly balanced. It is also a check point for the storage policy.

Traditional layouts

Find the warehouse layout that will provide the minimum operational costs (space + material handling). Limited to unit-load warehouses.
